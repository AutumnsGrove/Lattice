# Acceptable Use Policy

**Grove Platform**
**Effective Date:** December 10, 2025
**Last Updated:** December 10, 2025

---

## Community Standard

Grove is a space for authentic self-expression. We ask that you:

- Create content that contributes positively to your corner of the internet
- Respect others, even when you disagree
- Remember there's a real person on the other side of every screen

**Content that makes others feel unwelcome, unsafe, or targeted based on who they are has no place here.** When in doubt, ask yourself: *"Would I be comfortable if someone I respect saw this?"*

---

## 1. Prohibited Content

The following content is **not allowed** on Grove under any circumstances:

### 1.1 Illegal Content
- Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
- Content that promotes terrorism or violent extremism
- Content that facilitates illegal activities
- Content that violates applicable laws

### 1.2 Harassment and Threats
- Targeted harassment of individuals
- Doxxing (publishing private information without consent)
- Threats of violence or harm
- Stalking or intimidation
- Bullying or systematic abuse

### 1.3 Hate Speech
Content that attacks, demeans, or incites violence against individuals or groups based on:
- Race or ethnicity
- National origin
- Religion
- Gender or gender identity
- Sexual orientation
- Disability
- Age
- Any other protected characteristic

### 1.4 Spam and Malicious Content
- Spam, including repetitive or unsolicited promotional content
- Malware, viruses, or harmful code
- Phishing attempts or deceptive practices
- Automated abuse of the Service
- Content designed to manipulate search rankings

### 1.5 Impersonation and Fraud
- Impersonating another person or organization
- Creating fake accounts or identities
- Fraudulent schemes or scams
- Misrepresenting your affiliation or credentials

### 1.6 Explicit Sexual Content
- Pornography or sexually explicit material
- Graphic sexual content or "smut"
- Non-consensual intimate imagery

**Note:** Artistic nudity is permitted with appropriate content warnings. See Section 2.1.

### 1.7 Political Content
Grove is not a platform for political discourse. The following is prohibited:
- Political campaigning or advocacy
- Partisan political commentary
- Content promoting political candidates or parties
- Political fundraising

### 1.8 AI-Generated Content
- AI-generated text presented as original writing
- AI-generated images
- Deepfakes or synthetic media

Grove is a platform for authentic human expression. If you want to experiment with AI content, please use a platform designed for that purpose.

## 2. Content Requiring Warnings

### 2.1 Mature Content

The following content is **allowed** but must include appropriate content warnings:

**Artistic Nudity (Images)**
- Fine art, photography, or illustrations containing nudity
- Must be artistic in nature, not pornographic
- Images will have an automatic blur applied
- Viewers must click to reveal the content

**Mature Written Content**
- Content with mature themes (violence, adult situations, etc.)
- Suggestive or sensual content (not explicit)
- Must be marked with a content warning by the author
- Warning should appear before the content is visible

**How to Apply Content Warnings:**
When creating a post, use the content warning feature in the editor to mark mature content. The warning will be displayed to readers before they can view the full content.

## 3. Commercial Content

### 3.1 Promotional Posts

Commercial promotion is allowed with the following restrictions:

| Rule | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Frequency** | Maximum **one promotional post per 7 days** (rolling window) |
| **Location** | Blog posts only - promotional content is **not allowed** on Grove Social |
| **Definition** | Any content where you make money or sell something |

**Examples of promotional content:**
- "Check out my shop at [link]"
- Product announcements
- Affiliate links (must be disclosed)
- Sponsored content (must be disclosed)
- Partnership announcements
- Fundraising or crowdfunding campaigns
- Services you offer for hire

**Disclosure Requirements:**
- Affiliate links must be clearly disclosed
- Sponsored content must be labeled as such
- Paid partnerships must be acknowledged

### 3.2 Not Considered Promotional

The following is generally not considered promotional:
- Mentioning where you work in a personal context
- Sharing your portfolio or creative work
- Discussing products you personally use (without affiliate links)
- Linking to your other social media profiles

## 4. Copyright and Intellectual Property

### 4.1 Your Responsibilities
- Only post content you have the right to share
- Properly attribute content created by others
- Respect licenses and terms of use for third-party content
- Do not claim others' work as your own

### 4.2 DMCA Process
We respond to valid copyright complaints in accordance with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act. See our [DMCA Policy](./DMCA-POLICY.md) for details.

## 5. Enforcement

### 5.1 How We Enforce These Rules

**Detection:**
- User reports
- Automated scanning (privacy-respecting, anonymized, no human review unless flagged)
- Manual review of flagged content

**Actions We May Take:**
- Remove the violating content
- Issue a warning
- Temporarily suspend your account
- Permanently terminate your account

### 5.2 Enforcement Process

**For Account Violations:**

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1st violation | Warning |
| 2nd violation | Warning |
| 3rd violation | 1-week account suspension |
| 4th violation | Account termination |

After termination, you must wait **1 year** before you may appeal for a new account.

**For Severe Violations:**
We reserve the right to skip warnings and immediately suspend or terminate accounts for severe violations, including but not limited to:
- Illegal content
- Threats of violence
- CSAM
- Doxxing

### 5.3 Content Removal

When we remove content:

**DMCA Takedowns:**
- Content is removed as required by law
- No appeal is available for valid DMCA notices
- You may file a counter-notification if you believe the takedown was improper (see DMCA Policy)

**Policy-Based Removal:**
- You may appeal the removal
- **One appeal per removal**
- **Maximum 3 appeals per 7-day rolling period**
- We will respond to appeals within **7 business days**
- Our decision on appeal is final

### 5.4 Reporting Violations

If you see content that violates this policy, please report it by emailing autumn@grove.place with:
- A link to the content
- The specific rule you believe was violated
- Any additional context

We review all reports and take appropriate action. We do not disclose the identity of reporters.

## 6. Reserved Rights

### 6.1 Discretionary Enforcement
We reserve the right to remove any content or terminate any account at our discretion, even if not explicitly covered by these rules. We will exercise this discretion fairly and in accordance with our community values.

### 6.2 Misinformation
While we do not actively police misinformation at this time, we reserve the right to remove content that is demonstrably false and harmful. This policy may be expanded in the future as we develop appropriate tools and processes.

### 6.3 Policy Changes
We may update this policy as our community grows and evolves. Significant changes will be communicated to users via email.
