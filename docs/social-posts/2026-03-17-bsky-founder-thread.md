# I Built This For Us
**Platform:** Bluesky
**Date:** Monday, March 17, 2026
**Type:** Thread (7 posts)

---

**1.**
My name is Autumn. I'm a queer developer, and last month I launched something I built in about three months, entirely by myself. This is a little bit about why.

**2.**
The internet used to feel like an adventure. Now it mostly feels like standing in a loud mall looking for the exit. I wanted to build the exit. Or at least a door to somewhere quieter.

**3.**
Grove is what's through that door. A place for queer creators, independent writers, people who want a corner of the internet that actually belongs to them. Not a platform that tolerates you. A home.

**4.**
I wanted it to feel warm in the same way a good bookshop feels warm. Like an indie bookshop or a midnight tea shop, the kind of place where you stay longer than you planned and nothing is trying to sell you something.

**5.**
Building this alone meant a lot of nights where I wasn't sure it was working. I kept going because the people I was building it for are real, and I know what it feels like to need a space like this.

**6.**
It launched on February 14th. Not a bit, not marketing, that's genuinely just when it was ready. I'm still a little soft about that.

**7.**
If any of this resonates, if you've been looking for somewhere on the internet that feels like yours, the door is open. grove.place/hello #indieweb
