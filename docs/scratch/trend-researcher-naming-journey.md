# Naming Journey: Trend / Landscape Researcher

## What Is This Thing?

**Fundamentally:** A creature that watches the wider world — indie web communities, queer tech spaces, creator tool landscape — and brings back intelligence about what's happening, what's shifting, what opportunities are emerging.

**What does it DO?**
Scans the landscape: what are indie web folks talking about? What tools are gaining traction? What's WordPress doing that pushes people away? What do queer creators need that nobody's building? Surfaces trends, conversations, and opportunities that feed directly into content strategy and product decisions.

**What emotion should it evoke?**
Far-seeing, connected, tuned into currents others miss. Like someone who reads everything and tells you the three things that actually matter.

## Candidates

**Starling** ⭐
- Natural meaning: Famous for murmurations — sensing patterns in collective movement before anyone else. Migratory, adaptable, thrives everywhere.
- Why it fits: Trend research IS pattern sensing. Seeing the murmuration — the collective movement of indie web, queer tech, creator tools — and understanding where it's going before it gets there.
- The vibe: Connected, pattern-sensing, slightly ahead of the curve.

**Whale** 🐋
- Natural meaning: Sings across entire oceans, migrates vast distances, deeply social, ancient wisdom.
- Why it fits: "Surfacing" with insights from the deep internet.
- Potential issues: Too heavy/majestic for "go read some reddit threads."

## Selection: Starling

Starlings sense the murmuration — the collective movement that reveals where things are heading. They're also famously adaptable, thriving in any environment. And murmurations are BEAUTIFUL — patterns emerging from chaos. That's what good trend research does: finds the beautiful pattern in the noise.

**Workflow name:** The Murmuration

**Phases:**
```
FLOCK → SENSE → PATTERN → CALL → ROOST
```
