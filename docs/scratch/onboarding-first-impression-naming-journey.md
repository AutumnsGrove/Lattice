# Naming Journey: Onboarding / First Impression Tester

## What Is This Thing?

**Fundamentally:** A creature that sees your site with completely fresh eyes — like a stranger who just landed for the first time. Takes screenshots via Glimpse, analyzes them critically, and tells you what's confusing, what doesn't make sense, what's beautiful, and whether a new person would have any clue what's going on.

**What does it DO?**
Loads any Grove property, captures screenshots across devices/themes, and analyzes them from a "I've never been here before" perspective. "What is this site? What can I do here? Where do I go? Why should I care?" If the answers aren't obvious within 10 seconds, the rabbit flags it.

**What emotion should it evoke?**
Cautious, wide-eyed, noticing everything. The nervousness of arriving somewhere new. If anything is confusing or off-putting, you feel it immediately.

## Candidates

**Rabbit** 🐇
- Natural meaning: Cautious, alert, easily startled. Notices everything that feels "off." Huge eyes that take in the whole scene. If something is wrong, the rabbit bolts.
- Why it fits: The perfect first-impression tester. If the rabbit feels safe and welcome, the experience is good. If the rabbit is confused or startled, new users will be too. Rabbits are also incredibly common — like new visitors, they're everywhere but easily lost.
- The vibe: Wide-eyed, cautious, honest. "I don't understand what this button does and it makes me nervous."

**Fawn** 🦌
- Natural meaning: Young deer, seeing the world for the first time. Everything is new.
- Why it fits: Fresh eyes by definition. Ties to existing Deer (a11y) — the fawn is the young version.
- Potential issues: Too close to Deer. Could cause confusion.

## Selection: Rabbit

The rabbit's natural caution is EXACTLY what first-impression testing needs. Rabbits don't give you the benefit of the doubt. They notice the gap in the fence, the shadow that doesn't look right, the path that leads nowhere. If a rabbit can navigate your site and feel safe, anyone can.

**Workflow name:** The Warren

**Phases:**
```
ARRIVE → LOOK → WANDER → REPORT → BURROW
```
