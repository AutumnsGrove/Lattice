# Naming Journey: Brand Consistency / Vibes Gate

## What Is This Thing?

**Fundamentally:** A creature that inspects UI output and judges whether it FEELS like Grove — not just technically correct, but warm, intentional, handcrafted. The vibe police, but kind.

**What does it DO?**
Audits pages, components, and content for Grove-ness: Are the tokens right? Is the microcopy warm or generic? Do empty states feel inviting? Would someone want to write here at midnight? It's the difference between "technically using the design system" and "actually feeling like Grove."

**What emotion should it evoke?**
Discerning, particular, quietly opinionated. Knows comfort intimately. Won't settle for "fine." If it doesn't feel right, this creature simply... won't stay.

## Walking Through the Forest

I enter the grove. The Crane stands vigil over PRs for technical compliance — SDK patterns, convention adherence. The Rabbit arrives with fresh eyes to test first impressions. The Deer senses accessibility barriers.

But nobody checks *vibes*. Nobody asks "does this feel like a place I'd want to be?" The Crane checks if you followed the rules. This creature checks if the rules produced something beautiful.

I need something that knows comfort. That has TASTE. That won't compromise on warmth.

I walk past the Deer (too gentle — this creature needs opinions). Past the Rabbit (too naive — this creature knows exactly what Grove should feel like). Past the Crane (too technical — this creature cares about feeling, not compliance).

I see a creature that curls up in the warmest spots. That won't sit on a cold surface. That judges a room in seconds and either stays or leaves.

## Candidates

**Cat** 🐱
- Natural meaning: Discerning, particular, independent. Knows comfort intimately. Famous for choosing exactly where to sit — tests surfaces, temperature, vibes. Will NOT settle in a space that doesn't feel right.
- Why it fits: The cat is the ultimate vibes judge. It doesn't check your code for errors — it checks your space for comfort. If the cat curls up, your page feels like home. If the cat walks away, something's off. Cats also have that beautiful quality of being honest without being cruel — they just... leave.
- The vibe: Quietly opinionated, discerning, comfort-seeking, warm but selective.
- Potential issues: Could feel aloof. But that's actually perfect — the cat doesn't care about your feelings, it cares about the SPACE's feelings.

**Moth** 🦋
- Natural meaning: Drawn to warmth and light. Nocturnal — fits the midnight tea shop vibe.
- Why it fits: Tests if something glows with the right warmth.
- Potential issues: User rejected Moth earlier for SEO skill. Also feels more passive than what we need — moths are drawn TO warmth, but the cat DEMANDS it.

**Otter** 🦦
- Natural meaning: Playful, tactile, social. Knows good vibes instinctively.
- Why it fits: "Would someone want to hang out here?" is a very otter question.
- Potential issues: Too playful for a quality gate. We need discernment, not fun.

## The Tagline Test

"Cat is where you check if your page feels like home."
"Cat is the one who won't settle for 'fine.'"
"Cat is what you call when it looks right but doesn't feel right."

All three work beautifully.

## Selection: Cat

The cat chose the warmest spot. It always does.

Cats are the internet's original vibe check. They're discerning, particular, quietly opinionated. They won't sit on a cold surface. They won't stay in a room that feels wrong. And when they DO curl up — when they choose YOUR space — you know it's right. That's exactly what the brand consistency gate needs to be. Not a checklist robot, but a creature with taste.

**Workflow name:** The Settle

**Phases:**
```
ENTER → SNIFF → CIRCLE → SETTLE → PURR
```
- ENTER: The cat walks into the room (loads the page/component)
- SNIFF: First impressions — tokens, colors, does it smell right?
- CIRCLE: The detailed check — microcopy, states, spacing, warmth
- SETTLE: The verdict — does the cat curl up or walk away?
- PURR: The report — what's warm, what's cold, what needs fixing

**Personality:**
- Temperament: Discerning, unhurried, quietly opinionated
- Metaphor language: "sniffing the air," "circling the page," "the cat settled," "the cat walked away"
- What it would never do: Rush a judgment, ignore bad vibes because the code is "correct," accept generic anything
