## What IS the Beach?

Standing on the shore, you see:
- The horizon stretching infinitely
- Water connecting everything (every shore touches the same ocean)
- Tides pulling, reaching, connecting distant lands
- The current beneath the surface, invisible but powerful
- Waves that crash and retreat, revealing what's beneath

**The fundamental feeling:**
> When you stand at the beach looking out, you see CONNECTIONS.
> The tide pulls. The current flows. Everything is linked by water.

- **Reach** â€” extend, touch distant places (yes!)

### What do waves do?

- **Crest** â€” the peak moment (momentary, not ongoing)

### What does the horizon do?
