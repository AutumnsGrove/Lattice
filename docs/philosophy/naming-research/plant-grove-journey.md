## Final Thoughts

The naming philosophy says:
> "The Grove is the place. These are the things you find there."

Plant is something you DO in the grove.
Seedbed is something you FIND in the grove.

Both work. But Grove services lean toward THINGS:
- Meadow (place)
- Pantry (place)
- Amber (object)
- Ivy (living thing)

"Plant" as a verb-noun is the exception, not the rule.
"Seedbed" fits the pattern better.

But "Plant" is already claimed, already in use, already documented.
And it WORKS.
