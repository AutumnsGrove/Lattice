## The Decision

**Reeds wins.**

Here's why:

1. **The FEELING is right.** Comments should feel gentle, communal, whisperingâ€”not structural or architectural. Reeds evokes exactly that.

2. **Water is different.** Comments ARE a different element than the main post. The post is your tree. Comments are the reeds at your pond. Different, but connected.

3. **"Whisper together at the water's edge"** is a beautiful tagline that captures exactly what commenting should feel like in Grove.

4. **Threading can be rhizomatic.** Reeds spread through root systems. The visible reeds sway together; the connections happen beneath. That's actually a perfect metaphor for threaded conversations.

5. **It's already named Reeds.** And after this journey, I believe it's the RIGHT name.
