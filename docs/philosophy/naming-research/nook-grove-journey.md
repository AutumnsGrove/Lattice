## Okay. Decision Time.

The question was: Does Nook work? Or is there something better?

**My conclusion:**

Nook *works*. It's cozy, private, fits the Grove. But it undersells the GATHERING and VISUAL aspects of video sharing. It feels like curling up alone, not showing friends a video.

**Den** feels more natural for "gathering with your pack to watch videos."
It has the family movie night energy that matches the use case.

**Bower** is the most unique and poetic, capturing the visual display aspect.
But it requires more explanation.

If I had to choose:
1. **Den** for clarity and gathering energy
2. **Bower** for uniqueness and the visual/display connection
3. **Nook** if simplicity and existing momentum matter

---

## Final Thought: Maybe the Question Is...

Is this MORE about:

**A) The SPACE where you share** (Nook, Den, Hollow)
or
**B) The ACT of showing** (Bower, screening, projecting)

If it's about the space: **Den** wins (warm pack gathering).
If it's about the showing: **Bower** wins (curated visual display).

For a video sharing platform, the ACT of showing matters.
I lean **Bower**.

But **Den** is the safer choice. More intuitive.

After this journey, I think there are two strong paths:

### Path 2: Rename to Den

### Path 3: Rename to Bower
