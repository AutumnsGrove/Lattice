## What IS a Personal Roadmap?

Let me think about what this thing actually IS.

A roadmap is:
- A plan for where you're going
- Visible to others (building in public)
- Made of milestones (waypoints along the way)
- Forward-looking (the future!)
- Intentional (you CHOSE this path)

It's NOT:
- Just where you've been (that's history)
- Wandering (that's exploration)
- Private (this is public, shared)
- Accidental (this is deliberate)
