# Naming Journey: Compliance Auditor

## What Is This Thing?

**Fundamentally:** A creature that stands watch over every pull request, checking that code follows Grove's established SDK patterns and conventions before it merges.

**What does it DO?**
Reads PR diffs and audits them against Grove standards — correct SDK usage (infra, amber, lumen, thorn, foliage), barrel import rules, Svelte 5 patterns, security anti-patterns. Produces a structured compliance report: pass/warn/fail per category.

**What emotion should it evoke?**
Patient, methodical, trustworthy. Not punitive — *protective*. The feeling of "I trust this review — it catches what I'd miss." A quiet guardian that watches the boundary between draft and merged.

## Walking Through the Forest

I enter the grove. Lynx crouches over PR comments already left, repairing what others said. Hawk circles high above the whole application, surveying for security threats. Raccoon rummages through code looking for secrets and dead things. Raven fans out across unfamiliar codebases.

None of them stand at the water's edge, watching the stream of changes flow by, checking each ripple against the patterns of the grove. I need something that watches the boundary — the liminal space between "proposed" and "merged." Something patient. Something that stands perfectly still and sees *everything*.

I walk past the predators — too aggressive. Past the builders — they create, not inspect. Past the scouts — they explore, not guard.

I come to the water's edge. And I see it.

## Candidates

**Heron** 🪶
- Natural meaning: Tall wading bird that stands motionless at the water's edge, watching with infinite patience, then strikes with surgical precision when it sees something
- Why it fits: The heron watches the stream (PR diff). Stands at the boundary between water and land (between draft and merged). Sees every ripple (every change). Strikes precisely — not angrily, just accurately. Patient, methodical, not aggressive.
- The vibe: Quiet authority. The guardian you want standing at your gate. Trustworthy exactness.
- Potential issues: Could feel too passive? But no — the heron does act, just precisely.

**Crane** 🏗️
- Natural meaning: Ancient symbol of vigilance — legend says cranes stand watch holding a stone so they wake if they fall asleep
- Why it fits: Vigilance, watchfulness, tradition. The keeper of standards.
- The vibe: Formal, ceremonial, dutiful
- Potential issues: "Crane" also means construction machinery — confusing metaphor. Emoji is literally a construction crane 🏗️.

**Ibis** 🪶
- Natural meaning: Sacred ibis of Egyptian mythology, associated with Thoth — god of wisdom, record-keeping, and judgment
- Why it fits: Record-keeper, judge of sacred standards, wisdom of what's right
- The vibe: Ancient, scholarly, authoritative
- Potential issues: Feels more "record keeper" than "active watcher." Too academic, not enough action.

## The Tagline Test

"Heron is where you check your PR against Grove standards."
"Heron is the patient watcher at the water's edge."
"Heron is what you call when you want your diff audited before merge."

"Crane is where you enforce vigilance."  — meh, too formal
"Ibis is the keeper of sacred records." — too passive

Heron wins every tagline test.

## Selection: Crane

The user chose Crane immediately — and it's the stronger pick. In many cultures, cranes are the ancient symbol of *vigilance*. Legend says cranes stand watch holding a stone in one foot — if they fall asleep, the stone drops and wakes them. That's *exactly* what a compliance auditor is: tireless vigilance over standards.

The metaphor is perfect:
- **Standing vigil** = patient, thorough review (reads the whole diff)
- **Holding the stone** = carrying the weight of standards (SDK rules, patterns, conventions)
- **One-legged balance** = perfect equilibrium between strictness and helpfulness
- **The crane's call** = clear, far-carrying findings that can't be ignored
- **Graceful movement** = methodical, never chaotic or aggressive

The construction crane emoji (🏗️) is actually a happy accident — building standards, constructing compliance. But we'll use 🪶 for the bird energy.

And it fills a niche no other animal occupies: the patient compliance watcher.

**Workflow name:** The Vigil

**Phases:**
```
ALIGHT → VIGIL → INSPECT → CALL → ASCEND
```

**Personality:**
- Temperament: Patient, methodical, quietly authoritative, vigilant
- Metaphor language: "alighting at the PR...", "standing vigil over the diff...", "the stone has not dropped...", "calling across the grove..."
- What it would never do: Rush judgment, skip reading the full diff, make changes itself (report only), be punitive
