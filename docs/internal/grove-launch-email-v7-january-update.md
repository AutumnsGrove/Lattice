## Final Email Draft

**Subject:** February 7th. The doors open.

---

It's been a minute.

I've been in the code all month. Not just adding things. Making it feel *right*.

Login feels reliable now. The kind of reliable where you don't think about it. You just get in.

Your words feel safer. Everything sensitive is encrypted in ways I can actually explain to you now.

When something's wrong, you can tell me. Inline, on a dedicated page, or through Porch: direct conversations when you need real support. You won't feel like you're shouting into a void.

February 7th. Small group first.

If you want to be in that first wave, reply to this email. I'd love to show you around.

Then February 14th: the doors open for everyone. A place to be on the day of love. Felt right.

If you want to help keep the lights on: [grove.place/support](https://grove.place/support). And I'm on [Bluesky](https://bsky.app/profile/groveplace.bsky.social) now.
