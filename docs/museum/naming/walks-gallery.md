# The Walks Gallery

> *Every name in Grove was discovered, not invented. Here are the walks that found them.*

---

## What You're Looking At

This is the archive of every naming journey—the scratchpads, rejected ideas, and moments of clarity that built Grove's vocabulary. Each walk follows the same ritual: visualize the forest, ask what this thing *does*, and let the name emerge.

These are long documents. They meander. They double back. They argue with themselves. That's the point. Naming isn't a destination; it's a walk through the woods.

## How to Read These

Each walk follows a loose structure:

1. **The Question** — What needs naming?
2. **The Scene** — Visualizing where this thing lives in the grove
3. **The Walk** — Brainstorming, rejecting, discovering
4. **The Name** — What emerged, and why it fits

Some walks are short. Some meander for pages. The length often reflects how difficult the name was to find.
