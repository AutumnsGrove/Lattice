# The Aspen Wing

> *Why this exists, and why it was built this way.*

---

## What You're Looking At

This is the heart of the museum. Not architecture. Not code. Values.

Grove exists because someone needed a space and ended up building an entire world. This wing tells that story: the values that shaped every decision, the philosophy that makes this more than a blogging platform, and the identity that holds it all together.

Aspens are clonal colonies. One root system, many trunks. They share nutrients underground. They're the largest living organisms on Earth, not through dominance, but through connection. That's what Grove is. That's what Aspen means.

---

## Exhibits

### The Origin Story
From personal blog to platform. How "I need a website" became "I have an entire blogging platform." The AutumnsGrove repository, the moment of realization, the decision to tear it apart and rebuild it as an engine.

### The Values
Privacy-first. You own your content. You are your words. No algorithms. No data harvesting. No engagement optimization. Solarpunk, not cyberpunk. Community through connection, not competition.

### The Aspen Identity
Why aspens. Why clonal colonies. Why "resilient through connection, not armor." How the live deployment got its name and what it means for the platform's future.

### The Predecessor
The AutumnsGrove Museum, where this documentation pattern was born. Ten wings, ~3,400 lines, written for "Ariana, who wants to build her own site." The proof that code can be hospitable.
