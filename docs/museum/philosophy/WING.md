# The Philosophy Wing

> *How Grove names things, makes decisions, and builds with intention.*

---

## What You're Looking At

This wing preserves the meticulous process behind every decision in Grove. The naming ritual that turns "what does this thing do?" into a nature metaphor that genuinely fits. The design language that makes every page feel like a midnight tea shop. The world-building that means "feature flag" is called "Graft" and "user" is called "Wanderer."

Names aren't labels. They're world-building. And the process of finding them is a walk through the forest, not a brainstorming session with sticky notes.

---

## Exhibits

### The Naming Ritual
The process itself. How every name in Grove was discovered through a deliberate walk: visualize the forest, ask what this thing does in nature, let the name emerge. The philosophy behind "The Grove is the place. These are the things you find there."

### The Walks Gallery
56+ naming journeys, organized by category. The scratchpads, rejected ideas, and moments of clarity that built Grove's vocabulary. Three featured walks: Porch (the origin), Lumen (the difficult one), Loom (the one that returned home).

### The Ecosystem Map
60+ named services across the grove. Core infrastructure, creative tools, community spaces, standalone utilities. Each with its nature metaphor, its role, its story.

### The Design Language
Warm, introspective, queer, unapologetically building something meaningful. How the visual identity, the voice, and the interaction patterns all serve the same philosophy. Studio Ghibli warmth meets indie bookshop.
