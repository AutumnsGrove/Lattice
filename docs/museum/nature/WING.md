# The Nature Wing

> *The visual and emotional language of Grove.*

---

## What You're Looking At

This wing explains the design decisions that make Grove feel alive. Mathematically-driven SVG components create a living forest. Five seasons shift the mood. Glass surfaces layer over organic backgrounds. Gossamer weaves ASCII effects into translucent cards. Prism refracts design tokens into seasonal palettes.

---

## Exhibits

### [The Forest](./forest.md)
Trees, creatures, and weather effects that bring Grove to life.

### [The Seasons](./seasons.md)
Spring, Summer, Autumn, Winter, and Midnight.

### [The Glass Garden](./glass-garden.md)
Glassmorphism design that creates depth and warmth.

### [The Typography Gallery](./typography.md)
Accessible fonts respecting every reader.

### Gossamer
ASCII art effects library. 8 presets, domain warping, sparsity bias, alpha-by-brightness. The shimmer on glass cards, the static in the rain, the fireflies in the mist.

### Prism
Design tokens extracted into a zero-dependency library. Color palettes, icon systems, seasonal depth, contrast tools. Designed so icon packs can be swapped or extended. The light that passes through the glass.
