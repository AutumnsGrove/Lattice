# The AI Wing

> *Privacy-first AI that serves creators, not surveillance.*

---

## What You're Looking At

This wing explains how Grove uses AI without compromising on its values. Zero data retention. No training on your content. No lock-in to a single provider. AI that serves the creator, never the platform.

Every AI feature in Grove follows the same principle: your words belong to you. We analyze, suggest, protect, and configure, but we never store, never train, never sell. The AI is a tool in your workshop, not a landlord reading your diary.

---

## Exhibits

### Thorn
Content moderation with two layers: behavioral analysis first (deterministic, sub-millisecond), AI second (only when needed). Entity labels, rate checking, rule evaluation. The behavioral layer catches most abuse before AI is ever invoked.

### Reverie
The AI configuration pipeline. "Make my site cozy" becomes coordinated API calls across domain schemas. Five layers: Router, Loader, Converter, Composer, Validator. Natural language in, structured changes out.

### Lumen
Provider abstraction for AI inference. OpenRouter, Anthropic, Cloudflare AI, all behind one interface. Tool-calling support. Model fallbacks. The principle: no single vendor should own your intelligence.

### Songbird
Prompt injection protection. The careful boundary between user content and system instructions. Because when your AI writing assistant encounters "ignore previous instructions" in a blog post, it should proofread those words, not obey them.
