## What NOT to Do (Lessons from Competitors)

### Don't Pull a Bluesky
**What they did:** CEO responded "WAFFLES" to trans user raising safety concerns. Leadership dismissing marginalized users destroys trust instantly.

**Grove lesson:** Leadership must visibly care about safety. No jokes when users raise concerns.

### Don't Pull a Meta
**What they did:** Policy rollbacks to appease political pressure (January 2025).

**Grove lesson:** Values aren't negotiable. Don't compromise safety for growth.

### Don't Pull a Substack
**What they did:** Tolerate Nazi content for "free speech." Top writers left.

**Grove lesson:** Take clear stances. Don't "both sides" hate speech.

### Don't Pull a Cohost
**What they did:** Relied on single funder, no sustainable revenue model. Dead at 30K users.

**Grove lesson:** Have sustainable pricing from day one. Don't grow faster than you can fund.
