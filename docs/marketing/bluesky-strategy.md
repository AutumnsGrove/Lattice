## What Are Starter Packs?

Starter packs are curated lists of accounts users can follow all at once. They solve the "empty feed" problem and help communities find each other.

**Examples of LGBTQ+ starter packs:**
- 69+ Gay starter packs
- 135+ LGBTQIA+ general packs
- 19+ LGBTQ+ Artists packs
- 16+ LGBTQ+ Studies/academics packs
- 10+ Queer Artists packs
- Specialized: writers, game devs, musicians, photographers

**How to find them:** [blueskystarterpack.com/lgbtq](https://blueskystarterpack.com/lgbtq)

## Strategy: Three Phases

### Phase 1: Get INTO Existing Packs (Pre-Launch)

**Goal:** Get @groveplace (or your personal account) added to relevant starter packs before launch.

**Target packs to join:**
- Queer tech/indie web packs
- LGBTQ+ writers/bloggers packs
- Indie developers packs
- Solarpunk/ethical tech packs

**How to get added:**
1. Follow the pack creator
2. Engage genuinely with their content
3. Post about what you're building (building in public)
4. DM asking if they'd consider adding you (be genuine, not spammy)

**Your pitch:**
> "Hey! I'm building Grove, a queer-friendly blogging platform. Would love to be considered for your [pack name] if you think it fits!"

- Personal story moments ("Built this because I lost my hammock...")

## Metrics to Track

**What matters:**
- Click-throughs to grove.place
- Signups from Bluesky referrals
- Starter pack follows
- Genuine conversations started

**What doesn't matter:**
- Follower count
- Like counts
- "Engagement rate"

**How to track:**
- UTM parameters on links (`grove.place?ref=bluesky`)
- Ask in signup form: "How did you hear about Grove?"

## Emergency Playbook

### If a post goes viral:
1. Don't panic
2. Pin the most important link (grove.place)
3. Reply to genuine questions
4. Ignore trolls completely
5. It's okay to step away if overwhelmed

### If you get criticism:
1. **Valid criticism:** Thank them, consider it, maybe respond with your thinking
2. **Bad-faith attacks:** Don't engage, block if needed
3. **"Why should I pay for a blog?":** Have your answer ready (see competitor talking points)

### If Bluesky has drama:
Bluesky has had leadership issues (the "WAFFLES" incident). If something happens:
- Don't pile on
- Focus on your own values
- Maybe post: "This is why owning your own space matters"
