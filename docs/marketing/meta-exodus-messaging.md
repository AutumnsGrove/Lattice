# Meta Exodus Messaging

*Reaching LGBTQ+ users fleeing Instagram and Facebook after January 2025 policy changes*

---

## Context

**What happened:** On January 7, 2025, Meta announced major policy changes:
- Suspended fact-checking programs
- Reduced content moderation
- Updated hate speech policies to **allow calling transgender identity a "mental illness"**
- Removed "gender identity" from protected characteristics

**The impact:**
- 92% of LGBTQ+ respondents feel less protected
- 77% feel less safe expressing themselves
- Mass exodus to alternative platforms
- GLAAD launched "Make Meta Safe" campaign

**Where they're going:** Bluesky (primary), but many don't know where to go for *long-form* content. Instagram was visual/short-form; they need something new for writing.

**The opportunity:** Writers and creators who used Instagram/Facebook for updates and connection now need a *home base* that isn't controlled by Zuckerberg.
