## Notes

- Cloudflare Registrar offers **at-cost pricing** with no markup
- All domains include **free DNSSEC**
- Domain prices reflect only the registry wholesale fee plus ICANN fees
- Internationalized Domain Names (IDNs) are not currently supported
- Premium (non-standard priced) domains may have limited support
- Cloudflare continues to add new TLDs regularly, with focus on country-code TLDs
